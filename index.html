About Me

After earning a Marketing Degree from the College of Charleston I moved to London for a Sports Marketing Internship with a local children's soccer coaching company - “We Make Footballers.” While at “We Make Footballers”, I assisted with more than just marketing duties; being part of a small team I took part in many facets of the business, including web and creative design.

After the struggle that accompanied dealing with and finding the right freelance developers, having to do a little coding myself, and then seeing that the developer we used was working from Jamaica... I realized there was a need for efficient, clean, and effective developers. Upon returning from my Internship in London, I worked as a Mobile App Squad member for the NBA's Charlotte Hornets. I was able to combine my love of sports with my interest in tech, but knew I needed to know more about the tech side. Therefore, I gained a Full-Stack Developer certficate from The Coding Bootcamp at UNC Charlotte.
